Help Om Nom get his candy in Cut The Rope. Use your strategic skills to cut the ropes in the right order and collect stars in this fun and addictive puzzle game.

2048

Challenge your mind with 2048. Combine tiles with the same numbers to reach the coveted 2048 tile in this addictive and strategic puzzle game.

Chess

Sudoku

Solitaire

Enjoy the timeless card game Solitaire. Arrange the cards in order and suit to win the game in this classic and relaxing card game.
