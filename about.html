What we build

Schplay is a safe waypoint for finding classroom-friendly games, alternate mirrors, and incognito-ready experiences. We maintain fast global hosting, filter submissions, and collaborate with studios so more people can enjoy their creations.

We also publish updated how-to-play guides, document keyboard controls, and spotlight accessibility notes so players can jump in faster.

How we got here

In 2021 we bundled a few unblockable favorites for classmates. That list kept growing. By 2023, Schplay moved to dedicated hosting, added moderation, and began working with developers to keep classics alive.

Today the catalog spans retro flash ports, modern webGL builds, teacher-approved brain breaks, and spotlighted indie projects. The mission stays the same: give curious players a reliable, no-fuss place to explore.

What guides us

  • Access first. Games should work on school Wi-Fi, locked-down Chromebooks, and home networks alike.
  • Transparency. We tell you when a game is mirrored, when an ad supports a giveaway, and when a feature is in beta.
  • Community over clicks. Moderated chatrooms, code hints, and Discord office hours keep the vibe fun and collaborative.
  • Respect the creators. We link back, share original credits, and remove titles the moment a studio asks.
