Instructions

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game where players use logic to uncover safe squares while avoiding hidden mines. Test your problem-solving skills and aim for a perfect score!

Minesweeper has been a staple in the gaming world since its introduction in the 1980s. It has become a timeless classic, loved for its simplicity and challenging gameplay.

Start with the corners and edges to uncover safe squares. Use numbers to deduce the location of mines and mark them with flags. Plan your moves carefully to avoid mistakes.

Controls

  • Mouse — reveal tiles or place flags
  • Space — chord-click to clear safe squares
  • F2 — start a new board instantly
