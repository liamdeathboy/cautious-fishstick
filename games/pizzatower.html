Instructions

Pizza Tower is a lightning-fast platformer where you sprint, slam, and wall climb to stop the Pepperman. Score big by keeping combos alive!

Inspired by classic 90s platformers, Pizza Tower features expressive animation, secrets in every level, and an energetic soundtrack.

Chain moves together for huge scores: shoulder bash to keep momentum, dive to break blocks, and grab enemies for style points.

Controls

  • Arrow keys — move, crouch, and aim jumps
  • Z — jump and wall climb
  • X — dash, shoulder bash, and break blocks
  • C — grab enemies and interact
