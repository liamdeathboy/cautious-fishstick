Instructions

Geometry Dash is a fast-paced rhythm-based platformer game where players jump, fly, and flip through challenging levels. Test your reflexes and timing to complete each level!

Geometry Dash, developed by RobTop Games, has become a cultural phenomenon in the rhythm game genre. Its challenging gameplay and custom level editor have inspired a dedicated community of players and creators.

Focus on timing your jumps and movements to the rhythm of the music. Practice levels to memorize patterns and improve your performance. Use checkpoints in practice mode to master difficult sections.

Controls

  • Space / Up Arrow — jump to avoid spikes
  • Hold — fly ships smoothly through portals
  • Z / X — set practice checkpoints
