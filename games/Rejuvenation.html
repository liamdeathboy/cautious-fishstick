Undertale: Rejuvenation Mod (Web Version)

Play the Undertale: Rejuvenation Mod directly in your browser! This fan-made mod offers a fresh experience in the Undertale universe, featuring new characters, battles, and storylines while staying true to the spirit of the original game.

About Undertale: Rejuvenation

Undertale: Rejuvenation is a creative mod that expands on the classic Undertale gameplay. Encounter new monsters, explore unique areas, and make choices that influence the outcome of your journey. Whether you're a returning fan or new to Undertale mods, Rejuvenation offers a challenging and engaging adventure.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move and navigate menus.
  • Press Z/Enter to confirm or interact.
  • Press X/Shift to cancel or open the menu.
  • During battles, dodge attacks and choose your actions wisely.

Tips

  • Pay attention to new attack patterns and enemy behaviors unique to this mod.
  • Explore thoroughly to discover hidden secrets and story elements.
  • Try different choices for alternate endings and experiences.
