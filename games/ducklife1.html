Instructions

Duck Life 1 is the original duck training and racing game. Train your duck in various skills, compete in races, and aim to become the ultimate champion!

The Duck Life series started with this classic game, captivating players with its unique blend of training, strategy, and racing. It remains a fan favorite for its simplicity and charm.

Focus on training your duck in all skills to ensure balanced performance. Plan your races strategically and manage your resources wisely to achieve victory!

Controls

  • Mouse — feed your duck and buy seeds
