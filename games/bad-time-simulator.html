Bad Time Simulator (MAY TAKE A FEW MINTUE TO LOAD DO NOT LEAVE!)

Bad Time Simulator is a challenging Undertale-inspired game where you face off against Sans in an intense battle. Dodge attacks, survive, and test your skills in this thrilling experience!

Game History and Impact

Bad Time Simulator is a fan-made game inspired by the iconic Undertale series. It has gained popularity for its challenging gameplay and faithful recreation of the Sans fight, making it a favorite among fans.

Gameplay Strategies

Focus on learning attack patterns and timing your movements to avoid damage. Stay calm under pressure and practice to improve your reflexes. Perseverance is key to victory!
