About Our Ads

Advertising keeps Schplay free for students and lets us ship new classroom-friendly features every month. We keep it transparent, safe, and optional.

Updated March 2025
Our approach

Why you see ads on Schplay

We invest in moderating new games, building secure proxies, and hosting fast global mirrors. Ad revenue funds those services so every player can join without subscriptions. When you click an ad, it supports giveaways, new releases, and educator tools.

We partner only with networks that let us block sensitive categories and enforce COPPA-compliant targeting. Most placements are automated Google AdSense slots, with occasional direct sponsorships that we hand-review.

Current ad and analytics partners

Google AdSense

Delivers contextual ads and limits personalization for logged-out school devices. Opt out or customize at Google Ad Settings.

Google Analytics

Anonymous traffic insights that help us plan capacity and spotlight the games students love. IPs are truncated and retained for 26 months.

Direct sponsorships

Occasional events with STEM brands. Every creative is screened for school-appropriate messaging and energy.

How to control or remove ads

  • Signed-in Google users: Manage personalization through My Ad Center.
  • Educators: Email schools@schplay.com for an ad-light embed suitable for classroom projectors.
  • Browser controls: Limit cookies in your settings or use a privacy-focused browser. Core gameplay continues to work.
  • Pro users: We are exploring an ad-free supporter tier. Join the conversation in our Discord.

Ad standards & reporting

We reject creatives that include gambling, dating, or other topics unsuitable for schools. Placements are limited to clearly marked areas so you never mistake ads for gameplay. If you spot something that breaks our rules, send a screenshot and page URL to ads@schplay.com. We usually respond within one business day.
